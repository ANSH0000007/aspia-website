Who We Are

Dedicated to Healthcare.
Built for Excellence.

Welcome to Aspia Parenteral Private Limited — established with a commitment to healthcare excellence, we are a forward-thinking pharmaceutical manufacturing company based in Uttar Pradesh.

We specialize in the production of high-quality intravenous (IV) fluids and life-saving parenteral solutions, catering to hospitals, healthcare providers, and distributors across India.

Equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure at our Barabanki manufacturing plant, we leverage advanced FFS (Form-Fill-Seal) technology to ensure our products meet the highest standards of safety, sterility, and purity.

From normal saline to specialized dextrose solutions, every batch is manufactured under strict quality control guidelines and holds valid drug manufacturing licenses. Our goal is to bridge the gap between healthcare needs and affordable, reliable medical solutions.

Our Strengths

Manufacturing Excellence

What sets our facility apart and why healthcare providers trust us with their most critical supplies.

⚙️

FFS Technology

We employ advanced Form-Fill-Seal (FFS) technology — a globally trusted process for aseptic manufacturing that minimizes human intervention and maximizes sterility in every unit produced.

🔬

Strict Quality Control

Every batch of IV fluid undergoes rigorous in-process and final quality testing. Our QC protocols are aligned with current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP) to ensure product safety and reliability.

🏗️

State-of-the-Art Plant

Our modern manufacturing facility in Barabanki, UP, is designed with cleanroom environments, advanced automation, and compliant infrastructure to support large-scale, high-quality production.
